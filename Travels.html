Popular Travel Areas in India

Agra - Taj Mahal

Famous UNESCO World Heritage site in Uttar Pradesh.

New Delhi

Capital city with rich history and architecture.

Amritsar

Home to the iconic Golden Temple, a spiritual landmark.

Alleppey

Famous for the serene backwaters and houseboats in Kerala.

Manali

Popular hill station in Himachal Pradesh.

Mumbai

Financial capital with famous Gateway of India.

Kerala Backwaters

Network of tranquil canals, rivers and lakes.

Udaipur

City of lakes and palaces in Rajasthan.

Goa

Famous for beaches and nightlife.

Kolkata

Cultural capital of India with colonial history.

Khajuraho

Famous for exquisite temple sculptures.

Ladakh

High altitude desert with stunning landscapes.

Madurai

Known for historic Meenakshi Temple.

Mysore

Famous for royal heritage and palaces.

Meghalaya

Known for living root bridges and rainfall.

Sundarbans

Largest mangrove forest and tiger reserve.

Kanyakumari

Southernmost tip of India with sunrise views.

Srinagar

Famous for Dal Lake and houseboats.
